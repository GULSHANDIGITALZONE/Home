🔥 SSC MTS 2025 Online Apply करने का तरीका

Step 1: Official Website पर जाएं

ssc.gov.in खोलें। "New Registration" पर क्लिक करें।

Step 2: Registration करें

Basic Details भरें: Name, DOB, Email, Mobile। Captcha भरें। Submit करें।

Step 3: Login करें

Registration ID & Password से Login करें।

Step 4: Form भरें

  • Personal Details
  • Educational Qualification (10th Pass)
  • Address Details
  • Photo (20-50KB JPG) & Signature (10-20KB JPG)

Step 5: Fee Pay करें

General/OBC: ₹100 | SC/ST/Women: Free। Online (UPI/Card/Net Banking)।

Step 6: Preview & Submit

Form check करें। Final Submit। Print लें।

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Apply Date: Check ssc.gov.in (usually 3 months before exam)
  • Age: 18-25/27 Years
  • Exam: Paper 1 (CBT) + Paper 2
  • No Edit after Final Submit
