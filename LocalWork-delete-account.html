Account & Data Deletion Request

We at LocalWork value your privacy. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, you can do so either directly within the app settings or by following the instructions below.

What data will be deleted?

Upon your request, the following information will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • User Profile: Your name, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Job Postings: All jobs you have posted on the platform.
  • Messages: Your entire chat history with other users.

How to request deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)
Go to the Account section in the LocalWork app and click on "Delete My Account".

Option 2: Email Request
If you have already uninstalled the app, please email us at:
gulshandigitalzone@gmail.com

Note: Once processed, this action cannot be undone. It may take up to 7 business days to completely purge all data from our backups.
